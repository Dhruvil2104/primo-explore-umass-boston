How does UMBrella work?

UMBrella provides an easy-to-use, one-stop search and discovery tool for books, e-books, videos, articles, digital media, and so much more.

UMBrella also helps you manage your reasearch. Sign in using your UMass Boston network ID and password to:

  • Renew books and other library resources
  • Add items to your virtual bookshelf
  • Export citations to your assignments and research projects
  • View items and resources available at UMass Boston, as well as from other institutions
